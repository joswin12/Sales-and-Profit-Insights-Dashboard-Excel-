# Sales and Profit Insights Dashboard (Excel)


- Sheets used:
  - `Data` – raw transactional data with Revenue, Expenses, Profit, Category, Region, Department, Product Line, Customer Segment, Payment Method, and Discount.
  - `Pivot 1` to `Pivot 5` – supporting pivot tables for the dashboard.
  - `Dashboard` – final interactive dashboard built with Excel charts, slicers, and pivot tables.

## Problem Statement

The goal of this dashboard is to help the business understand how revenue, expenses, and profit are distributed across regions, departments, product lines, and customer segments. By centralizing key KPIs in a single Excel dashboard, decision makers can quickly identify high‑performing areas, underperforming combinations, and opportunities to optimize profitability.

## Data Overview

- Transactions: ~2,000+ rows of sales records with IDs from `TXN0001` onward.  
- Time period: Transactions from 2022–2023.  
- Key fields:
  - Financials: Revenue, Expenses, Profit.
  - Business dimensions: Category, Region, Department, Product Line.
  - Customer dimensions: Customer Segment (B2B, B2C, SMB, Enterprise), Payment Method (Cash, Credit Card, Bank Transfer, PayPal).
